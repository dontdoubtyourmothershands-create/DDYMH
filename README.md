# DDYMH
Don’t Doubt Your Mother’s Hands LLC (DDYMH) is a New York City–wide childcare company designed to support families who need care outside traditional hours. We provide safe, reliable, and inclusive 24-hour / overnight childcare options for parents and caregivers with non-traditional schedules, emergencies, or flexible care needs. Our model focuses on trust, routines, safety, and community-centered support across all five boroughs.
